After leaving the Primary School due to going to Secondary, I moved to Friern Barnet School, I spent only 2 months there, but it was okay-ish. We had to move, because of personal problems, leading me to go to the last school which I spent the rest of my time there and it was probably the worst but the best looking, worst because of the kids and I didn't really fit in properly. The school is called Teddington School. After that, we moved back to the Czech Republic and I have been living here since.
